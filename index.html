they trust that source.

Anderson Cooper of CNN was the personality that Democrats and Republicans disagreed over the

most. With 65% of Democrats trusting him compared to only 14% of Republicans.

 

But Cooper’s overall trustworthiness among Americans was higher than usual with 35% of US Adults
